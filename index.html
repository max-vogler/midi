Web MIDI Experiments

Interactive experiments that augment DJ software with web technology. Run these in your browser and connect them to your favorite DJ software and MIDI hardware. Read the source code and add your own experiment at github.com/max-vogler/midi.

/VIZ/ Visualize an external MIDI beat clock
/TOUCH/ Control effects with touch & pressure
/GATEWAY/ Forward MIDI to another device with WebRTC
/CONTROLLER/ Control music with gamepads
/HANDPOSE/ Control effects with your hand mid-air
/LATENCY/ Measure latency of a loopback MIDI bus
